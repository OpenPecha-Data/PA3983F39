|5,1|

priyebhyo jāyate śokaḥ priyebhyo jāyate bhayam |priyāṇām anyathī bhāvād unmādam api gacchati
|5,2|

śokā hi ye vai paridevitaṃ ca duhkhaṃ ca lokasya hi naikarūpam |priyaṃ pratītyeha tad asti sarvaṃ
priye ’sati syān na kathaṃcid etat |5,3|

tasmādd hi te sukhitā vītaśokā yeṣāṃ priyaṃ nāsti kathaṃcid eva |tasmād aśokaṃ padam eṣamāṇaḥ
priyaṃ na kurvīta hi jīvaloke |5,4|

mā priyaiḥ saṃgamo jātu mā ca syād apriyaiḥ sadā |priyāṇām adarśanaṃ duhkham apriyāṇāṃ ca dharśanam
|5,5|

priyāṇāṃ ca vinā bhāvād apriyāṇāṃ ca saṃgamāt |tīvra utpadyate śoko jīryante yena mānavāḥ |5,6|

priyaṃ mṛtaṃ kālagataṃ jñātayaḥ sahitāḥ sthitāḥ |śocanti dīrgham adhvānaṃ duhkho hi priyasaṃgamaḥ
|5,7|

tasmāt priyaṃ na kurvīta priyabhāvo hi pāpakaḥ |granthās teṣāṃ na vidyante yeṣāṃ nāsti priyāpriyam
|5,8|

ayuge yujya cātmānaṃ yuge cāyujya sarvadā |arthaṃ hitvā priyagrāhī spṛhayaty arthayogine |5,9|

priyarūpasātagrathitā devakāyāḥ pṛthaksthitāḥ |āghādinaḥ paridyūnā mṛtyu rājavaśaṃ gatāḥ |5,10|
