abhūtavādī narakān upaiti yaś cānyad apy ācaratīha karma |ubhau hi tau pretyasamau niruktau
nihīnadharmau manujau paratra |8,1|

puruṣasya hi jātasya kuṭhārī jāyate mukhe |yayā chinatti hātmānaṃ vācā durbhāṣitaṃ vadan |8,2|

yo nindiyāṃ praśaṃsati tān api nindati ye praśaṃsiyāḥ |sa cinoti mukhena taṃ kaliṃ kalinā tena
sukhaṃ na vindati |8,3|

alpa mātro hy ayaṃ kalir ya ihhākṣeṇa dhanaṃ parājayet |ayam atra mahattaraḥ kalir yaḥ sugateṣu
manaḥ pradūṣayet |8,4|

śataṃ sahasrāṇi nirarbudāni ṣaṭ triṃśatiṃ pañca tathārbudāni |yān āryagarhī narakān upaiti vācaṃ
manaś ca praṇidhāya pāpakam |8,5|

asataṃ hi vadanti pāpacittā narakaṃ vardhayate vadhāya nityam |anavadyabalas titīkṣate tāṃ manaso
hy āvilatāṃ vivarjayitvā |8,6|1

yaḥ śāsanaṃ hy arhatāṃ āryāṇāṃ dharmajīvinām |pratikrośati durmedhā dṛṣṭiṃ nihśritya pāpikām
|8,7|

kalyāṇikāṃ vimuñceta naiva muñceta pāpikām |muktā kalyāṇikī śreyo muktā tapati pāpikā |8,8|

na ca mukte pramuñcet tāṃ muñcamāno hi bādhyate |naivaṃ āryāḥ pramuñcanti muktā bālair hi pāpikā
|8,9|

mukhena samyato bhikṣur mandabhāṣī hy anuddhataḥ |arthaṃ dharmaṃ ca deśayati madhuraṃ tasya
bhāṣitam |8,10|
