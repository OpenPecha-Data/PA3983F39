na hi vaireṇa vairāṇi śāmyantīha kadācana |kṣāntyā vairāṇi śāmyanti eṣa dharmaḥ sanātanaḥ |14,11|

vairaṃ na vaireṇa hi jātu śāmyet śāmyed avaireṇa tu vairabhāvaḥ |vairaprasaṅgo hy ahitāya dṛṣṭas
tasmādd hi vairaṃ na karoti vidvān |14,12|

sa cel labhed vai nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ loke caran sādhu hi nityam eva |abhibhūya sarvāṇi parisravāṇi
careta tenāptamanā smṛtātmā |14,13|

no cel labhed vai nipakaṃ sahāyaṃ loke caran sādhu hi nityam eva |rājeva rāṣṭraṃ vipluaṃ prahāyaikaś
caren na ca pāpāni kuryāt |14,14|

caraṃś ca nādhigaccheta sahāyaṃ tulyaṃ ātmanaḥ |ekacaryāṃ dṛḍhaṃ kuryān nāsti bāle sahāyatā
|14,15|

ekasya caritaṃ śreyo na tu bālaḥ sahāyakaḥ |ekaś caren na ca pāpāni kuryād alpotsuko ’raṇyagata
iva nāgaḥ |14,16|

ekasya caritaṃ śreyo na tu bāla sahāyatā |alpotsukaś cared eko mātaṅgāraṇye nāgavat |14,16|
