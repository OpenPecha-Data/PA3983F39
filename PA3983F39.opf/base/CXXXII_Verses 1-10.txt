piṇḍacārikāya bhikṣave hy ātmabharāya hi nānyapoṣiṇe |devāḥ spṛhayanti tāyine hy upaśāntāya
sadā smṛtātmane |32,1|

piṇḍapātikāya bhikṣave hy ātmabharāya hi nānyapoṣiṇe |devāḥ spṛhayanti tāyine na tu satkārayaśo
’bhikāṅkṣine |32,2|

sarvakarmajahasya bhikṣuṇe dhunvānasya puraskṛtaṃ rajaḥ |amamasya sadā sthitātmano hy artho
nāsti janasya lāpanam |32,3|

tudanti vācābhir asamyatā janāḥ śarair hi saṃgrāmagataṃ yathā gajam |śrutvā tu vācāṃ paruṣām
udīritām adhivāsayed bhikṣur aduṣṭacittaḥ |32,4|

yas tv alpajīvī laghur ātmakāmo yatendriyaḥ sarvagatiḥ pramuktaḥ |anokasārī hy amamo nirāśaḥ
kāmaṃ jahaś caikacaraḥ sa bhikṣuḥ |32,5|

mātraṃ bhajeta pratirūpaṃ śuddhājīvo bhavet sadā |pratisaṃstāravṛttiḥ syād ācārakuśalo bhavet
|tataḥ pramodyabahulaḥ smṛto bhikṣuḥ parivrajet |32,6|

hastasamyataḥ pādasamyato vācā samyataḥ sarvasamyataḥ |ādhyātmarataḥ samāhito hy ekaḥ saṃtuṣito
hi yaḥ sa bhikṣuḥ |32,7|

dharmārāmo dharmarato dharmam evānucintayan |dharmaṃ cānusmaran bhikṣur dharmān na parihīyate
|32,8|

śunyāgāraṃ praviṣṭasya prahitātmasya bhikṣuṇaḥ |amānuṣā ratir bhavati samyag dharmān vipaśyataḥ
|32,9|

yato yataḥ saṃspṛśati skandhānām udayavyayam |prāmodyaṃ labhate tatra prītyā sukham analpakam
|tataḥ prāmodyabahulaḥ smṛto bhikṣuḥ parivrajet |32,10|
