anekāni sahasrāṇi nara nārī śatāni ca |bhogān vai samudānīya vaśaṃ gacchanti mṛtyunaḥ |1,21|

sarve kṣayāntā nicayāḥ patanāntāḥ samucchrayāḥ |samyogā viprayogāntā maraṇāntaṃ hi jīvitam |1,22|

sarve sattvā mariṣyanti maraṇāntaṃ hi jīvitam |yathā karma gamiṣyanti puṇya pāpa phala upagāḥ
|1,23|

narakaṃ pāpa karmāṇaḥ kṛta puṇyās tu sad gatim |anye tu mārgaṃ bhāvyeha nirvāsyanti nirāsravāḥ
|1,24|

naivāntarīk se na samudra madhye na parvatānāṃ vivaraṃ praviśya |na vidyate ’sau pṛthivī pradeśo
yatra sthitaṃ na prasaheta mṛtyuḥ |1,25|

ye ceha bhūtā bhaviṣyanti vā punaḥ sarve gamiṣyanti prahāya deham |tāṃ sarva hāniṃ kuśalo viditvā
dharme sthito brahmacaryaṃ careta |1,26|

jīrṇaṃ ca dṛṣṭveha tathaiva rogiṇaṃ mṛtaṃ ca dṛṣṭvā vyapayāta cetasam |jahau sa dhīro gṛha bandhanāni
kāmā hi lokasya na supraheyāḥ |1,27|

jīryanti vai rāja rathāḥ sucitrā hy atho śarīram api jarām upaiti |satāṃ tu dharmo na jarām
upaiti santo hi taṃ satsu nivedayanti |1,28|

dhik tvām astu jare grāmye virūpa karaṇī hy asi |tathā mano ramaṃ bimbaṃ jarayā hy abhimarditam
|1,29|

yo ’pi varṣa śataṃ jīvet so ’pi mṛtyu parāyaṇaḥ |anu hy enaṃ jarā hanti vyādhir vā yadi vāntakaḥ
|1,30|
