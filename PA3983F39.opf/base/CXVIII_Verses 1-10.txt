ka imāṃ pṛthivīṃ vijeṣyate yamalokaṃ ca tathā sadevakam |ko dharmapadaṃ sudeśitaṃ kuśalaḥ puṣpam
iva praceṣyate |18,1|

śaikṣaḥ pṛthivīṃ vijeṣyate yamalokaṃ ca tathā sadevakam |sa hi dharmapadaṃ sudeśitaṃ kuśalaḥ
puṣpam iva praceṣyate |18,2|

vanaṃ chindata mā vṛkṣaṃ vanād vai jāyate bhayam |chittvā vanaṃ samūlaṃ tu nirvaṇā bhavata bhikṣavaḥ
|18,3|

na chidyate yāvatā vanaṃ hy anumātram api narasya bandhuṣu |pratibaddhamanāḥ sa tatra vai vatsaḥ
kṣīrapaka iva mātaram |18,4|

ucchinddhi hi snehaṃ ātmanaḥ padmaṃ śāradakaṃ yathodakāt |śānti mārgam eva bṛṃhayen nirvāṇaṃ
sugatena deśitam |18,5|

yathāpi ruciraṃ puṣpaṃ varṇavat syād agandhavat |evaṃ subhāṣitā vācā niṣphalāsāv akurvātaḥ |18,6|

yathāpi ruciraṃ puṣpaṃ varṇavat syāt sugandhavat |evaṃ subhāṣitā vācā saphalā bhavati kurvataḥ
|18,7|

yathāpi bhramaraḥ puṣpād varṇagandhāv aheṭhayan |paraiti rasaṃ ādāya tathā grāmān muniś caret
|18,8|

na pareṣāṃ vilomāni na pareṣāṃ kṛtākṛtam |ātmanas tu samīkṣeta samāni viṣamāni ca |18,9|

yathāpi puṣparāśibhyaḥ kuryān mālā guṇān bahūn |evaṃ jātena martyena kartavyaṃ kuśalaṃ bahu
|18,10|
