śīlaṃ rakṣeta medhāvī prārthayan vai sukhatrayam |praśaṃsāvittalābhaṃ ca pretyasvarge ca modanam
|6,1|

sthānāny etāni sampaśyan śīlaṃ rakṣeta paṇḍitaḥ |āryo darśanasampannaḥ sa loke labhate śivam
|6,2|

sukhaṃ śīlasamādānaṃ kāyo na paridahyate |sukhaṃ ca rātrau svapati pratibhuddhaś ca nandati
|6,3|

śīlaṃ yāvaj jarā sādhu śraddhā sādhu pratiṣṭhitā |prajñā narāṇāṃ ratnaṃ vai puṇyaṃ coraiḥ sudurharam
|6,4|

kṛtvā puṇyāni saprajño dattvā dānāni śīlavān |iha cātha paratrāsau sukhaṃ samadhigacchati |6,5|

śīle pratiṣṭhito bhikṣur indriyaiś ca susaṃvṛtaḥ |bhojane cāpi mātrajño yukto jāgarikāsu ca
|6,6|

viharann evaṃ ātāpī hy aho rātram atandritaḥ |abhavyaḥ parihāṇāya nirvāṇasyaiva so ’ntike |6,7|

śīle pratiṣṭhito bhikṣuś cittaṃ prajñāṃ ca bhāvayet |ātāpī nipako nityaṃ prāpnuyād duhkhasaṃkṣayam
|6,8|

tasmāt satataśīlī syāt samādher anurakṣakaḥ |vipaśyanāyāṃ śikṣec ca samprajānapratismṛtaḥ |6,9|

sa tu vikṣīṇasamyogaḥ kṣīṇamāno niraupadhiḥ |kāyasya bhedat saprajñaḥ saṃkhyān nopaiti nirvṛtaḥ
|6,10|
