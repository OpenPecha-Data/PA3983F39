udagracittaḥ sumanā hy abhibhūya priyāpriyam |prāmodyabahulo bhikṣur duhkhakṣayam avāpnuyāt

śāntakāyaḥ ... śāntavāksusamāhitaḥ |vāntalokāmiṣo bhikṣur upaśānto nirucyate |32,24|

|32,26|

||adhicitte samāyogaṃ yasyāsau bhikṣur ucyate |32,27|

|32,28|

|32,29|

ihaiva yaḥ prajānāti duhkhasya kṣayaṃ ātmanaḥ |kalyāṇaprajñaṃ āhus taṃ sadā śīlam anāsravam
