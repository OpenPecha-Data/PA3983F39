sarvapāpasyākaraṇaṃ kuśalasyopasampadaḥ |svacitta paryavadanam etad buddhasya śāsanam |28,1|

dadataḥ puṇyaṃ pravardhate vairaṃ na kriyate ca samyamāt |kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ rāgadoṣamohakṣayāt
tu nirvṛtiḥ |28,2|

sārdhaṃ carann ekakaḥ sadā miśro hy anyajanena vedakaḥ |kuśalī prajahāti pāpakaṃ krauñcaḥ kṣīrapako
yathodakam |28,3|

dṛṣṭvā hy ādīnavaṃ loke jñātvā dharmaniraupadhim |āryo na ramate pāpe pāpo na ramate śubhe |28,4|

pravivekarasaṃ jñātvā rasaṃ copaśamasya vai |nirjvaro bhavati niṣpāpo dharmaprīti rasaṃ piban
|28,5|

anavasrutacittasya tv anunahana cetasaḥ |puṇyapāpaprahīṇasya nāsti durgatito bhayam |28,6|

niṣeddhāraṃ pravaktāraṃ yaj jāned vadyadarśinam |nigṛhyavādinaṃ dhīraṃ tādṛśaṃ satataṃ bhajet
|tādṛśaṃ bhajamānasya śreyo bhavati na pāpakam |28,7|

upaśānto hy uparato mandabhāṣī hy anuddhataḥ |dhunāti pāpakān dharamān drumapattraṃ yathānilaḥ
|28,8|

yo hy apraduṣṭasya narasya duṣyate śuddhasya nityaṃ vigatāṅgaṇasya |tam eva bālaṃ pratiyāti
pāpaṃ kṣiptaṃ rajaḥ prativātaṃ yathaiva |28,9|

yad yat karoti puruṣas tat tat paśyati hātmanaḥ |kalyāṇakārī kalyāṇaṃ pāpakārī ca pāpakam |28,10|
