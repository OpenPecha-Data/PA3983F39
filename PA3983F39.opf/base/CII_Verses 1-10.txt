kāma jānāmi te mūlaṃ saṃkalpāt kāma jāyase |na tvāṃ saṃkalpayiṣyāmi tato me na bhaviṣyasi |2,1|

kāmebhyo jāyate śokaḥ kāmebhyo jāyate bhayam |kāmebhyo vipramuktānāṃ nāsti śokaḥ kuto bhayam
|2,2|

ratibhyo jāyate śoko ratibhyo jāyate bhayam |ratibhhyo vipramuktānāṃ nāsti śokaḥ kuto bhayam
|2,3|

madhurāgrā vipāke tu kaṭukā hy abhinanditāḥ |kāmā dahanti vai bālān ulkevāmuñcataḥ karam |2,4|

na tad dṛḍhaṃ bandhanaṃ āhur āryā yad āyasaṃ dāravaṃ balbajaṃ vā |saṃrakta cittasya hi manda
buddheḥ putreṣu dāreṣu ca yā avekṣā |2,5|

etad dṛḍhaṃ bandhanaṃ āhur āryāḥ samantataḥ susthiraṃ duṣpramokṣam |etad api chittvā tu parivrajanti
hy anapekṣiṇaḥ kāma sukhaṃ prahāya |2,6|

na te kāmā yāni citrāṇi loke saṃkalpa rāgaḥ puruṣasya kāmaḥ |tiṣṭhanti citrāṇi tathaiva loke
athātra dhīrā vinayanti cchandam |2,7|

na santi nityā manujeṣu kāmāḥ santi tv anityāḥ kāmino yatra baddhāḥ |tāṃs tu prahāya hy apunar
bhavāya hy anāgataṃ mṛtyu dheyaṃ vadāmi |2,8|

chanda jāto hy avasrāvī manasānāvilo bhavet |kāmeṣu tv apratibaddha citta ūrdhva sroto nirucyate
|2,9|

anupūrveṇa medhāvī stokaṃ stokaṃ kṣaṇe kṣaṇe |karmāro rajatasyaiva nirdhamen malaṃ ātmanaḥ |2,10|
