pūrvaṃ hi kṛtyaṃ pratijāgareta mā me kṛtyaṃ kṛtyakāle vihanyāt |taṃ nityakāle pratiyatyakāriṇaṃ
naiva kṛtyaṃ kṛtyakāle vihanti |16,1|

vyāyametaiva puruṣo yāvad arthasya niṣpadaḥ |paśyed asau tathātmārthaṃ yathaivecchet tathā bhavet
|16,2|

uttiṣṭhata vyāyamata kurudhvaṃ dvīpaṃ ātmanaḥ |karmāro rajatasyaiva haradhvaṃ malaṃ ātmanaḥ
|nirdhāntamalā hy anaṅgaṇā na punar jāti jarām upeṣyatha |16,3|

alajjitavye lajjante lajjitavye tv alajjinaḥ |abhaye bhayadarśīno bhaye cābhayadarśinaḥ |mithyā
dṛṣṭi samādānāt sattvā gacchanti durgatim |16,4|

yas tu pūrvaṃ pramādyeha paścād vai na pramādyate |sa imaṃ bhāsate lokam abhramukta iva candramāḥ
|16,5|

yas tu pūrvaṃ pramādyeha paścād vai na pramādyate |sa imāṃ viṣaktikāṃ loke smṛtaḥ samativartate
|16,6|

daharo ’pi cet pravrajate yujyate buddhaśāsane |sa imaṃ bhāsate lokam abhramukta iva candramāḥ
|16,7|

daharo ’pi cet pravrajate yujyate buddhaśāsane |sa imāṃ viṣaktikāṃ loke smṛtaḥ samativartate
|16,8|

yasya pāpakṛtaṃ karma kuśalena pithīyate |sa imaṃ bhāsate lokam abhramukta iva candramāḥ |16,9|

yasya pāpakṛtaṃ karma kuśalena pithīyate |sa imāṃ viṣaktikāṃ loke smṛtaḥ samativartate |16,10|
