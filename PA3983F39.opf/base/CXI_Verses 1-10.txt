yat kiṃcit śithilaṃ karma saṃkliṣṭaṃ vāpi yat tapaḥ |apariśuddhaṃ brahmacaryaṃ na tad bhavati

śaro yathā durgṛhīto hastam evāpakṛntati |śrāmaṇyaṃ duṣparāmṛṣṭaṃ narakān upakarṣati |11,4|

śaro yathā sugṛhīto na hastam apakṛntati |śrāmaṇyaṃ suparāmṛṣṭaṃ nirvāṇasyaiva so ’ntike |11,5|

duṣkaraṃ dustitīkṣaṃ ca śrāmaṇyaṃ mandabuddhinā |bahavas tatra sambādhā yatra mando viṣīdati
|11,6|

|11,7|

|11,9|
