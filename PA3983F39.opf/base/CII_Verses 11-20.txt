ratha kāra iva carmaṇaḥ parikartann upānaham |yad yaj jahāti kāmānāṃ tat tat sampadyate sukham
|2,11|

sarvaṃ cet sukham iccheta sarva kāmāṃ parityajet |sarva kāma parityāgī hy atyantaṃ sukham edhate
|2,12|

yāvat kāmān anusaran na tṛptiṃ manaso ’dhyagāt |tato nivṛttiṃ pratipaśyamānās te vai tṛptāḥ
prajñayā ye sutṛptāḥ |2,13|

śreyasī prajñayā tṛptir na hi kāmair vitṛpyate |prajñayā puruṣaṃ tṛptaṃ tṛṣṇā na kurute vaśam
|2,14|

gṛddhā hi kāmeṣu narāḥ pramattā hy adharme bata te ratāḥ |antarāyaṃ na te paśyanty alpake jīvite
sati |2,15|

durmedhasaṃ hanti bhogo na tv ihātma gaveṣiṇam |durmedhā bhoga tṛṣṇābhir hanty ātmānam atho
parān |2,16|

na karṣāpaṇa varṣeṇa tṛptiḥ kāmair hi vidyate |alpāsvāda sukhāḥ kāmā iti vijñāya paṇḍitaḥ |2,17|

api divyeṣu kāmeṣu sa ratiṃ nādhigacchati |tṛṣṇā kṣaya rato bhavati buddhānāṃ śrāvakaḥ sadā
|2,18|

parvato ’pi suvarṇasya samo himavatā bhavet |vittaṃ taṃ nālam ekasyaitaj jñātvā samaṃ caret
|2,19|

duhkhaṃ hi yo veda yato nidānaṃ kāmeṣu jantu sa kathaṃ rameta |upadhiṃ hi loke śalyam iti matvā
tasyaiva dhīro vinayāya śikṣet |2,20|
