na tāvatā dharmadharo yāvatā bahu bhāṣate |yas tv ihālpam api śrutvā dharmaṃ kāyena vai spṛśet
|sa vai dharmadharo bhavati yo dharme na pramādyate |4,21|

subahv apīha sahitaṃ bhāṣamāṇo na tat karo bhavati naraḥ pramattaḥ |gopaiva gāḥ saṃgaṇayan pareṣāṃ
na bhāgavān śrāmaṇyārthasya bhavati |4,22|

alpam api cet sahitaṃ bhāṣamāṇo dharmasya bhavati hy anudharmacārī |rāgaṃ ca doṣaṃ ca tathaiva
mohaṃ prahāyabhāgī śrāmaṇyārthasya bhavati |4,23|

apramādaṃ praśaṃsanti pramādo garhitaḥ sadā |apramādena maghavān devānāṃ śreṣṭhatāṃ gataḥ |4,24|

apramādaṃ praśaṃsanti sadā kṛtyeṣu paṇḍitāḥ |apramatto hy ubhāv arthāv atigṛhṇāti paṇḍitaḥ |4,25|

dṛṣṭadhārmika eko ’rthas tathānyaḥ sāmparāyikaḥ |arthābhisamayād dhīraḥ paṇḍito hi nirucyate
|4,26|

apramādarato bhikṣuḥ pramāde bhayadarśakaḥ |durgād uddharate tmānaṃ paṅka sanna iva kuñjaraḥ
|4,27|

apramādarato bhikṣuḥ pramāde bhayadarśakaḥ |dhunāti pāpakāṃ dharmāṃ pattrāṇīva hi mārutaḥ |4,28|

apramādarato bhikṣuḥ pramāde bhayadarśakaḥ |samyojanam aṇu sthūlaṃ dahann agnir iva gacchati
|4,29|

apramādarato bhikṣuḥ pramāde bhayadarśakaḥ |spṛśati hy anupūrveṇa sarvasamyojanakṣayam |4,30|
