sādhu śrutaṃ sucaritaṃ sādhu cāpy aniketatā |pradakṣiṇaṃ pravrajyā ca śrāmaṇyasyānulomikam |22,1|

bālā ihāvijānantaś caranti hy amarā iva |vijānatāṃ tu sad dharmaṃ āturasyaiva śarvarī |22,2|

yathā hy agāraṃ succhannaṃ praviśya tamasā sphuṭam |vidyamānāni rūpāṇi cakṣuṣmān hi na paśyati
|22,3|

tathaiveha naro nityaṃ jñānavān api yo bhavet |aśrutvā na vijānāti dharmān kalyāṇapāpakān |22,4|

pradīpena tu rūpāṇi cakṣuṣmān paśyate yathā |evaṃ śrutvā vijānāti dharmān kalyāṇapāpakān |22,5|

śrutvā dharmān vijānāti śrutvā pāpaṃ na sevate |śrutvā hy anarthaṃ varjayate śrutvā prāpnoti
nirvṛtim |22,6|

bahu śruto ’pi ced bhavati śīleṣu tv asamāhitaḥ |śīlatas taṃ vigarhanti nāsya sampadyate śrutam
|22,7|

alpaśruto ’pi ced bhavati śīleṣu susamāhitaḥ |śīlatas taṃ praśaṃsanti tasya sampadyate śrutam
|22,8|

alpaśruto ’pi ced bhavati śīleṣu tv asamāhitaḥ |ubhayatas taṃ vigarhanti nāsya sampadyate vratam
|22,9|

bahu śruto ’pi ced bhavati śīleṣu susamāhitaḥ |ubhayatas taṃ praśaṃsanti tasya sampadyate vratam
|22,10|
