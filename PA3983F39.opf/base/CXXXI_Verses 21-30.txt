manaḥ pūrvaṃgamā dharmā manaḥśreṣṭhā manojavāḥ |manasā hi praduṣṭena bhāṣate vā karoti vā |tatas
taṃ duḥkham anveti cakraṃ vā vahataḥ padam |31,23|

manaḥ pūrvaṃgamā dharmā manaḥśreṣṭhā manojavāḥ |manasā hi prasannena bhāṣate vā karoti vā |tatas
taṃ sukham anveti chāyā vā hy anugāminī |31,24|

nāprasannena cittena duṣṭena kṣubhitena vā |dharmo hi śakyaṃ ājñātuṃ saṃrambhabahulena vā |31,25|

vinīya yas tu saṃrambham aprasādaṃ ca cetasā |āghātaṃ caiva nihsṛjya prajānīyāt subhāṣitam |31,26|

na pratyanīkasāreṇa suvijñeyaṃ subhāṣitam |upakliṣṭena cittena saṃrambhabahulena vā |31,27|

anavasthitacittasya saddharmam avijānataḥ |pāriplavaprasādasya prajñā na paripūryate |31,28|

srotāṃsi yasya ṣaṭ triṃśan manaḥ prasravaṇāni hi |vahanti nityaṃ durdṛṣṭeḥ saṃkalpair gredhanihśritaiḥ
