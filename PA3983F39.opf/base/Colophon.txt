uddānam ||

anityakāmatṛṣṇā cāpramādas tathā priyaḥ |śīlaṃ sucaritaṃ vācakarmaśraddhā ca te daśaḥ ||śramaṇo
mārgasatkāro drohasmṛti prakīrṇakaḥ |udakaṃ puṣpam aśvaś ca saha krodhena te daśaḥ ||tathā
gataḥ śrutaṃ cātmā peyālaṃ mitrapañcamam |nirvāṇaṃ paśya pāpaṃ ca yugavargaḥ sukhena
ca |cittaṃ bhikṣur brāhmaṇaś ca trayas triṃśatime smṛtāḥ |vargāḥ samāptāś coddānaṃ samyak
sambuddhabhāṣitāḥ |
