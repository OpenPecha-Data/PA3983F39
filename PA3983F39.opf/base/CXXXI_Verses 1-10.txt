durnigrahasya laghuno yatra kāma nipātinaḥ |cittasya damanaṃ sādhu cittaṃ dāntaṃ sukhāvaham
|31,1|

vārijo vā sthale kṣipta okād oghāt samuddhṛtaḥ |parispandati vai cittaṃ māradheyaṃ prahātavai
|31,2|

pṛthag vidhāvate cittaṃ sūryasyeva hi raśmayaḥ |tat paṇḍito vārayati hy aṅkuśenaiva kuñjaram
|31,3|

bhrūṇadheyam idaṃ cittaṃ nihsāram anidarśanam |sadainam anuśāsāmi mā mānarthāya niścaret |31,4|

idaṃ purā cittam acāri cārikāṃ yenecchakaṃ yena kāmaṃ yatheṣṭam |tat samnigṛhṇāmi hi yoniśas
tv idaṃ nāgaṃ prabhinnaṃ hi yathāṅkuśena |31,5|

anekaṃ jāti saṃsāraṃ saṃdhāvitvā punaḥ punaḥ |gṛhakārakaiṣamāṇas tvaṃ duhkhā jātiḥ punaḥ punaḥ
|31,6|

gṛhakārakadṛṣṭo ’si na punar gehaṃ kariṣyasi |sarve te pārśukā bhagnā gṛhakūṭaṃ visaṃskṛtam
|visaṃskāragate citte ihaiva kṣayam adhyagāḥ |31,7|

spandanaṃ capalaṃ cittaṃ durakṣyaṃ durnivāraṇam | ṛjuṃ karoti medhāvī iṣu kāra iva tejasā |31,8|

dūraṃ gamam ekacaram aśarīraṃ guhāśayam |ye cittaṃ damayiṣyanti vimokṣyante mahābhayāt |31,8A|

na dveṣī dveṣiṇaḥ kuryād vairī vā vairiṇo hitam |mithyā praṇihitaṃ cittaṃ yat kuryād ātmanātmanaḥ
|31,9|

na taṃ mātā pitā vāpi kuryāj jñātis tathāparaḥ |samyak praṇihitaṃ cittaṃ yat kuryādd hitaṃ ātmanaḥ
