jayād vairaṃ prasavate duhkhaṃ śete parājitaḥ |upaśāntaḥ sukhaṃ śete hitvā jayaparājayau |30,1|

paraduhkhopadhānena ya icchet sukhaṃ ātmanaḥ |vairasaṃsargasaṃsakto duhkhān na parimucyate |30,2|

sukhakāmāni bhūtāni yo daṇḍena vihiṃṣati |ātmanaḥ sukham eṣāṇaḥ sa vai na labhate sukham |30,3|

sukhakāmāni bhūtāni yo daṇḍena na hiṃsati |ātmanaḥ sukham eṣāṇaḥ sa pretya labhate sukham |30,4|

dharmaṃ caret sucaritaṃ nainaṃ duścaritaṃ caret |dharmacārī sukhaṃ śete hy asmin loke paratra
ca |30,5|

dharmaḥ sadā rakṣati dharmacāriṇaṃ chatraṃ mahad varṣakāle yathaiva |eṣānuśaṃso dharme sucīrṇe
na durgatiṃ gacchati dharmacārī |30,6|

dharmaḥ sadā rakṣati dharmacāriṇaṃ dharmaḥ sucīrṇaḥ sukhaṃ ādadhāti |eṣānuśaṃso dharme sucīrṇe
na durgatiṃ gacchati dharmacārī |30,7|

alpāpi santo bahavo jayanti susaṃvidhāne na saṃvidhānam |alpam api cet śraddadhāno dadāti tenaivāsau
bhavati sukhī paratra |30,8|

dānaṃ ca yuddhaṃ ca samānaṃ āhur naite guṇāḥ kāpuruṣeva santi |saṃgrāma veleva hi dānavelā tulyaṃ
bhavet kāraṇa saṃgraheṇa |30,9|

ayaṃ hi pratyūhaśatāni jitvā mātsaryaṃ ākramya ca śatru bhūtam |śurādd hi taṃ śūrataraṃ vadāmi
dadāti yo dānam asaktacittaḥ |30,10|
