sarvābhibhūḥ sarvavid eva cāsmi sarvaiś ca dharmaiḥ satataṃ na liptaḥ |sarvaṃ jahaḥ sarvabhayād
vimuktaḥ svayaṃ hy abhijñāya kam uddiśeyam |21,1|

kam uddiśeyaṃ tv asamo hy atulyaḥ svayaṃ pravaktā hy adhigamya bodhim |tathāgato devamanuṣya
śāstā sarvajñatāṃ prāpya balair upetaḥ |21,2|

ahaṃ hi lokeṣv arahann ahaṃ lokeṣv anuttaraḥ sadevakeṣu lokeṣu cāhaṃ mārābhibhūr jinaḥ |21,3|

ācāryo me na vai kaścit sadṛśas ca na vidyate |eko ’smin loke sambuddhaḥ prāptaḥ sambodhim uttamām
|21,4|

jinā hi mādṛśā jñeyā ye prāptā hy āsravakṣayam |jitā me pāpakā dharmās tato ’ham upagā jinaḥ
|21,5|

bārāṇasīṃ gamiṣyāmi haniṣyāmṛtadundubhim |dharmacakraṃ pravartayiṣye lokeṣv aprativartitam |21,6|

na hi santaḥ prakāśyante viditvā lokaparyāyam |ādeśayanto virajaḥ padaṃ śāntamanīṣiṇaḥ |21,7|

nadantīha mahāvīraḥ sad dharmeṇa tathāgatāḥ |dharmeṇa nadamānānāṃ ke tv asūyed vijānakāḥ |21,8|

ye dhyānaprasṛtā dhīrā naiṣkramyopaśame ratāḥ |devāpi spṛhayanty eṣāṃ buddhānāṃ śrīmatāṃ sadā
|21,9|

teṣāṃ devā manuṣyāś ca sambuddhānāṃ yaśasvinām |spṛhayanty āśu buddhīnāṃ śarīrāntimadhāriṇām
|21,10|
